About


I am Angel Sara Mathew student of BathSpa University of Creative computing
here showcasing my works in the portfolio
This is a website about the crafts that I have made. "Globe of Imagination"

refers to the ideas created in our minds by our imagination. Global of Imagination'
brings the works of my imagination to life.
What I wish to say using this title is that there is a world
where every imagination can be made visible to others,
through which people can travel to imaginary worlds,
experiencing the difference and uniqueness of each person's mind.
This is why the title is known as the Globe of Imagination.
